MS Dhoni : The legend

About MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicket-keepers and finishers in the history of the game.

Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), Dhoni made his international debut in 2004 and went on to play 538 international matches, scoring over 17,000 runs and taking 829 dismissals behind the stumps.

Achievements

  • ICC World Cup winner (2011)
  • ICC World Twenty20 winner (2007)
  • Indian Premier League (IPL) champion (2010, 2011, 2018)
  • Recipient of the Padma Bhushan, India's third-highest civilian honor (2018)

Quotes

  • "I don't mind repeating myself, but I don't want to repeat my mistakes."
  • "You don't play for the crowd, you play for the country."
